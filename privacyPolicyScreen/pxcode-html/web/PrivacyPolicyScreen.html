Rumango Ltd’s purpose in collecting non-personally identifying information is to better understand how Rumango Ltd’s visitors use its website. From time to time, Rumango Ltd may release non-personally-identifying information in the aggregate, e.g., by publishing a report on trends in the usage of its website. 

To enrich and perfect your online experience, Rumango Ltd uses "Cookies", similar technologies and services provided by others to display personalized content, appropriate advertising and store your preferences on your computer.

A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor’s computer, and that the visitor’s browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. Rumango Ltd uses cookies to help Rumango Ltd identify and track visitors, their usage of http://Rumango Ltd.com, and their website access

We have no control over, and assume no responsibility for the content, privacy policies or practices of any third party sites, products or services.

The security of your Personal Information is important to us, but remember that no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your Personal Information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.
